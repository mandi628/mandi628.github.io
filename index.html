About

After years as a stay-at-home mom, raising four kids, Mandi went to work as a freelance musician and teacher. Always passionate and curious about computers, when her youngest graduated from high school, Mandi set about learning the craft of web and software development.

She is now a freelance web developer and programmer, and enjoys the freedom of location this type of work offers. In her spare time, Mandi continues to teach private music lessons, performs in various venues in the Southeast, and spins her own wool for knitting, crocheting, felting, weaving, and more. 📓
